Last night at The Anchor, we hosted an unforgettable Gameshow House Party, led by the fabulous drag queen, Nikki Manfadge. From 7:30pm to 11pm, the pub was filled with non-stop laughter and joy, creating a night to remember for everyone. Our cosy village pub in Stanwell Moor Village was transformed into a lively hub of entertainment, welcoming everyone for an evening of fun and games.

  

## **A Night of Laughter and Fun with Nikki Manfadge**

As the clock struck 7:30pm, Nikki Manfadge took the stage, dazzling everyone with her vibrant personality and witty humour. Nikki’s presence was magnetic, drawing everyone in with her infectious energy. The evening began with a lively game of drag bingo. The room buzzed with excitement as numbers were called, and winners were treated to shots and bottles of wine. The crowd’s cheers and laughter filled the pub, setting the perfect tone for the night.

  

## **Hilarious Games and Heartwarming Moments**

Next up was Blankety Blank. Armed with whiteboards, our guests scribbled down their funniest answers, sparking waves of laughter with every round. The atmosphere was electric, with everyone joining in the fun. Nikki’s playful banter kept the energy high, and the audience didn’t disappoint with their creative and hilarious responses. The highlight of the evening was the Mr and Mrs game. Couples took the stage, ready to reveal amusing and endearing secrets about their relationships, making us laugh and warm our hearts at the same time.
